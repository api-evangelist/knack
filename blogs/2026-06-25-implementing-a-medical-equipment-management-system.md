A medical equipment management system (MEMS) is a centralized system for tracking, maintaining, and managing medical devices throughout their lifecycle. Healthcare organizations use a medical equipment management system to keep critical equipment organized, available, and operating as expected. Many organizations use MEMS software to bring equipment inventories, maintenance records, inspection schedules, and service histories into a single source of truth.
